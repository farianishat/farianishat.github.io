About Me

Hi there! 👋

I’m a Lecturer in the department of Computer Science and Engineering at Daffodil International University, where I get to combine my love for teaching, research, and technology every day. I believe a teacher gets to shape a world through shaping the mind of a student. I wish to be that world changing teacher one day.

One of my proudest roles is serving as the Convener of DIU Girls' Computer Programming Club(GCPC) where I and my girls work to prepare the future tech geniuses.

Outside of teaching and research, I like to sing, recite, debate and anchor events. I feel alive on stage and enjoy every second of time I get to talk and present something I am passionate about.

Research Interest

I am a Machine Learning Enthusiast. I have publications in Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing and Health Informatics. In future I have a plan to explore more applied fields of Machine learning. Security, Privacy, Reinforcement Learning and Multimodal Agents are such areas I would love to work on.
